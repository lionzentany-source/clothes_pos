Place your fallback fonts here, e.g. NotoNaskhArabic-Regular.ttf and NotoNaskhArabic-Bold.ttf
Suggested paths:
	- assets/fonts/NotoNaskhArabic-Regular.ttf
	- assets/fonts/NotoNaskhArabic-Bold.ttf

You can auto-download them on Windows using:
	scripts/get_noto_fonts.ps1

After adding the files:
	1) Open pubspec.yaml and uncomment the "Noto Naskh Arabic" fonts section.
	2) Run flutter pub get
	3) Rebuild the app
